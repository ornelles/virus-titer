##
## Redefine variable names
##

mfi - fluorescence (signal) intensity replacing "val" or "value"

x - the dependent value to be used for Poisson fitting, typically this is the multiplicity of infection, volume, or particle number replacing "moi".

unit - unit description for x such as "VP", "IU", "ul", or "ml"

well - identifies images from the same biological unit. The word "well" is reflects the typically usage where the biological unit is a single well of a multi-well dish. The image will either be paired images or a stack of paired images.

xm, ym - x, y location of nuclear mask as center of mass or median in pixels

area - area of nuclear mask in pixels

dna - fluorescence intensity of DNA stain

dir - enclosing directory name for all image files

file - filename or pathname to image file within dir

positive - logical value indicating if cell is positive
